ZedZapper — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ZedZapper ("we", "the app") handles information. By using the app you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

ZedZapper does not ask for or collect your name, email, or account details. The app stores your game progress (high score, level reached, and ad-removal purchase) locally on your device.

Advertising and third parties

ZedZapper displays ads through Unity LevelPlay (ironSource) and its ad networks, including Unity Ads. To deliver and measure ads, these partners may access your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) and collect device and usage data. On iOS we request your permission through the App Tracking Transparency prompt before any tracking takes place; you can decline and still play the game, and you can change this at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking. We do not personalize ads without your consent. For details, see the Unity / ironSource privacy policy.

Purchases

In-app purchases (Remove Ads, Go to Level) are processed by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment details.

Children

ZedZapper is not directed at children under 4, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your region, you may have the right to access or delete data held about you, and to opt out of personalized advertising or the sale of personal information. You can use the App Tracking Transparency and iOS privacy controls, or contact us using the details below.
